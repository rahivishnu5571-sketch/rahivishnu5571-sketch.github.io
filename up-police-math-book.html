Chapter 1 – Number System

Important Concepts

  • LCM × HCF = Product of two numbers
  • Divisibility Rules
  • Prime Factorization
Example 1: Find HCF of 36 and 48.
Solution: 36 = 2×2×3×3 48 = 2×2×2×2×3 HCF = 2×2×3 = 12
Example 2: Find LCM of 12 and 15.
Solution: 12 = 2×2×3 15 = 3×5 LCM = 2×2×3×5 = 60

Chapter 2 – Percentage

Formula: Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

Example 1: Find 25% of 200.
Solution: (25/100) × 200 = 50
Example 2: Increase 400 by 10%.
Solution: 10% of 400 = 40 New value = 400 + 40 = 440

Chapter 3 – Profit and Loss

Profit = SP − CP
Loss = CP − SP

Example 1: CP = 500, SP = 600
Profit = 100 Profit% = (100/500)×100 = 20%
Example 2: CP = 800, SP = 720
Loss = 80 Loss% = (80/800)×100 = 10%

Chapter 4 – Ratio and Proportion

Ratio compares two quantities.

Example: If 2:3 = x:12, find x.
2/3 = x/12 x = 8

Chapter 5 – Simple Interest

Formula: SI = (P×R×T)/100

Example: P=1000, R=5%, T=2 years
SI = (1000×5×2)/100 = 100
