SSC GD Constable 2026 – Complete Preparation Guide

By India Study Point | Published on: 17 February 2026

SSC GD Constable 2026 is one of the most competitive government examinations conducted by the Staff Selection Commission. Every year lakhs of candidates apply for the General Duty Constable posts in CAPFs, NIA, SSF and Assam Rifles. To succeed in this exam, candidates need a clear understanding of the exam pattern, syllabus and physical test requirements along with a smart preparation strategy.

SSC GD 2026 Selection Process

The recruitment process generally includes the following stages:

  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Physical Standard Test (PST)
  • Medical Examination

Candidates must qualify in all stages to secure final selection.

SSC GD 2026 CBT Exam Pattern

The Computer Based Test consists of objective type multiple-choice questions. The subjects included are:

  • General Intelligence and Reasoning
  • General Knowledge and General Awareness
  • Elementary Mathematics
  • English / Hindi

The exam includes negative marking, so accuracy is very important.

Detailed Subject-wise Preparation Strategy

1. General Intelligence & Reasoning

This section tests logical thinking ability. Important topics include analogy, coding-decoding, series, blood relations and puzzles. Practice at least 25 reasoning questions daily to improve speed.

2. General Knowledge & Current Affairs

Focus on current affairs of the last 6–12 months. Also prepare static GK such as history, geography, Indian polity and basic science.

3. Elementary Mathematics

Important topics include percentage, ratio and proportion, profit and loss, time and work, average, algebra and geometry. Revise formulas regularly and solve practice questions daily.

4. English / Hindi

Prepare grammar rules, vocabulary, synonyms, antonyms and comprehension passages. Daily reading improves language skills.

Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

Candidates who qualify CBT must clear the Physical Efficiency Test. Running is an important part of PET. Regular physical exercise and stamina building are essential to pass this stage.

Best Study Plan for SSC GD 2026

  • 2 hours – Mathematics
  • 1.5 hours – Reasoning
  • 1.5 hours – GK & Current Affairs
  • 1 hour – Language
  • 1 hour – Revision and mock test analysis

Consistency is more important than studying for long hours occasionally. Daily focused study ensures better results.

Importance of Mock Tests

Mock tests help in understanding exam pattern and time management. Attempt at least two full-length mock tests per week and analyze mistakes carefully.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ignoring physical preparation
  • Not revising regularly
  • Skipping mock tests
  • Studying without a fixed timetable

Conclusion

SSC GD Constable 2026 offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for a secure government job. With proper planning, dedication and regular practice, clearing this examination is achievable. Stay disciplined, stay consistent and work towards your goal with confidence.
