How to Prepare for SSC CGL 2026 – Complete Guide

SSC CGL is one of the most popular government exams in India. Every year lakhs of students apply for it. If you are preparing for SSC CGL 2026, this guide will help you.

  • Tier 2 – Objective Test

Subjects in SSC CGL

Preparation Strategy

1. Make a proper study timetable.

2. Practice previous year papers.

3. Revise daily.

4. Attempt mock tests weekly.

Final Tips
