Osho – Mrityu Shiksha (Summary)

Introduction

Osho teaches that death is not an end but a transformation. Fear of death comes from misunderstanding life.

Understanding Death

According to Osho, when a person becomes aware through meditation, fear disappears. Death becomes a doorway to another state of existence.

Spiritual Insight

Life and death are part of one continuous flow. Awareness brings acceptance and peace.

Conclusion

Mrityu Shiksha encourages understanding life deeply so that death is no longer frightening.
