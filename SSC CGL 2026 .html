SSC CGL 2026 Preparation Strategy

Complete Syllabus, Study Plan & Book List

Introduction

SSC CGL 2026 is one of the most popular government exams in India. Lakhs of students apply every year. If you follow the right preparation strategy and consistent practice, you can clear this exam easily.

SSC CGL Exam Pattern

Tier 1 Subjects

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Awareness
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • English Comprehension

Tier 2 Subjects

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • English Language
  • Reasoning
  • General Studies

6 Month Study Plan

Follow this simple daily schedule:

  • 2 hours – Mathematics
  • 2 hours – Reasoning
  • 1 hour – English
  • 1 hour – General Awareness
  • 1 hour – Revision & Mock Test

Best Books for SSC CGL

  • Quantitative Aptitude – Rakesh Yadav
  • Reasoning – Kiran Publication
  • English – Plinth to Paramount
  • General Knowledge – Lucent GK

Important Preparation Tips

  • Solve previous year question papers.
  • Attempt mock tests weekly.
  • Focus on weak subjects.
  • Revise daily formulas and vocabulary.

Conclusion

SSC CGL preparation needs consistency and smart work. Make a timetable and follow it strictly. With dedication and practice, selection is possible.
