Railway Latest Job 2026 – Overview

Indian Railways is one of the largest employers in the country. Every year, lakhs of candidates apply for Railway recruitment exams. The Railway Latest Job 2026 notification is expected to include thousands of vacancies across various zones.

  • Organization: Indian Railways
  • Conducting Body: Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)
  • Posts: Group D, NTPC, ALP, Technician, JE
  • Mode of Exam: Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Job Location: Across India

Railway Vacancy 2026 Post Details

1. RRB Group D 2026

Group D recruitment includes Level-1 posts such as Track Maintainer, Helper, Assistant Pointsman and other technical support roles. This is one of the most popular railway exams.

2. RRB NTPC 2026

Non-Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) include posts like Clerk, Goods Guard, Station Master and Commercial Apprentice.

3. RRB ALP & Technician 2026

Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) and Technician posts are technical roles requiring ITI or diploma qualifications.

4. RRB Junior Engineer (JE)

Junior Engineer recruitment is conducted for diploma and engineering graduates in various technical branches.

Railway Recruitment 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Educational qualification varies according to the post:

  • Group D – 10th Pass / ITI
  • NTPC – 12th Pass / Graduate
  • ALP & Technician – ITI / Diploma
  • JE – Diploma / Engineering Degree

Age Limit

Minimum age is generally 18 years. Maximum age ranges between 30 to 33 years depending on the post. Age relaxation is applicable for SC/ST/OBC and other reserved categories as per government norms.

Railway Selection Process 2026

The Railway recruitment selection process usually consists of multiple stages:

  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Second Stage CBT (if applicable)
  • Skill Test / Typing Test (for specific posts)
  • Physical Efficiency Test (for Group D)
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

Railway Exam Pattern 2026 (CBT)

The Computer Based Test includes objective type multiple-choice questions. The main subjects are:

  • Mathematics
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Science
  • General Awareness & Current Affairs

Negative marking is applicable for incorrect answers. Therefore, candidates must focus on accuracy along with speed.

How to Apply Online for Railway Latest Job 2026

  1. Visit the official RRB website of your region.
  2. Click on “Latest Recruitment 2026” notification.
  3. Complete the registration process using valid details.
  4. Fill in personal, educational and communication details.
  5. Upload required documents (photo, signature, certificates).
  6. Pay the application fee online.
  7. Submit the application form and download confirmation.

Preparation Strategy for Railway Recruitment 2026

Make a daily timetable and allocate time for each subject. Consistency is more important than studying randomly.

Previous year papers help understand exam trends and difficulty level.
