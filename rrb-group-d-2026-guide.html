RRB Group D 2026 – Complete Preparation Guide

RRB Group D 2026 is one of the most popular railway recruitment exams in India. Every year lakhs of candidates apply for this examination to secure a government job in Indian Railways. The selection process includes a Computer Based Test (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), document verification and medical examination.

RRB Group D 2026 Exam Overview

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) conducts Group D recruitment for various posts such as Track Maintainer, Helper, Assistant Pointsman and other Level-1 posts. Candidates must carefully understand the exam pattern and syllabus before starting preparation.

Selection Process

  • Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

RRB Group D 2026 CBT Exam Pattern

  • General Science
  • Mathematics
  • General Intelligence and Reasoning
  • General Awareness and Current Affairs

The CBT exam is objective type (multiple choice questions). Time management and accuracy are very important because negative marking is applicable.

Detailed Syllabus

1. Mathematics

Important topics include Number System, Percentage, Ratio and Proportion, Profit and Loss, Simple Interest, Time and Work, Time and Distance, Algebra and Geometry.

2. General Intelligence and Reasoning

Questions are based on coding-decoding, analogy, series, puzzles, blood relations and syllogism.

3. General Science

Physics, Chemistry and Biology up to 10th standard level are usually asked.

4. General Awareness

Current affairs, Indian history, geography, polity, economics and railway related updates are important for this section.

Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

Candidates who qualify CBT are called for PET. Male and female candidates have different physical standards. Regular physical training is necessary to clear this stage.

Best Preparation Strategy for RRB Group D 2026

Create a Study Plan

Make a daily timetable and divide time for each subject. Consistency is the key to success.

Solve Previous Year Papers

Practicing previous year papers helps in understanding the difficulty level and important topics.

Attempt Mock Tests

Weekly mock tests improve speed, accuracy and confidence.

Revision

Daily revision of formulas and important facts is essential to score well.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ignoring physical preparation
  • Not practicing mock tests
  • Skipping revision
  • Lack of time management

Conclusion

RRB Group D 2026 is a great opportunity for candidates who want to join Indian Railways. With proper planning, regular practice and physical fitness preparation, candidates can easily clear this exam. Stay consistent and keep working towards your goal.
